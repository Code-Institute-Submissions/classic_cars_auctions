How do I bid?

You can do this for free on our website by registering an account.

If an item you're interested in you can place a Bid, in order to buy it you'll have to bid on and "win". To do so, enter value into the box (your bid) and click "Place Bid."

Before you decide to bid, however, there are some things you should know:

Our auctions accept bids only for a specific amount of time. In a traditional auction, bidders frantically place competing bids. When bidding slows to nothing, the auctioneer pounds the gavel and the item is sold. On CCA, auctions are open to bids for exactly 1 day. When time is up, the high bidder wins, even if people are still frantically bidding.

If your bid wins, you must buy. Your bid on an auction is a legally binding contract. If, when time runs out, your bid is the highest, you have purchased the item and must pay the for it.

Is there a guarantee on my purchase?

No, cars are sold on an as seen basis. You are advised to satisfy yourself as to the vehicles condition and suitability before bidding. We offer viewing opportunities, as well as photographic content for each car offered.
